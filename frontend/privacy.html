Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Closet Org (“we”, “the app”) is a personal wardrobe organizer operated by Rohin Patel. This policy describes what we collect when you use the mobile app or website, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account: email, username, and a hashed password when you register.
  • Closet data: photos you upload, item metadata (category, colors, style, season), wear history, outfits, trips, and preferences you enter in the app.
  • Location (optional): if you allow it, approximate location is used only to fetch local weather for outfit suggestions. You can deny location permission and still use the app.
  • Technical: standard server logs (IP address, request time, user agent) for security and debugging.

How we use information

  • Provide closet storage, classification, recommendations, and social features you enable.
  • Authenticate your session and protect your account.
  • Improve reliability and fix errors.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for third-party advertising.

Where data is stored

Your account and closet data are stored on servers we operate (currently a single cloud virtual machine). Photos and database records remain on that infrastructure unless you delete them or delete your account.

Sharing

We do not share your closet with other users unless you use in-app social features (e.g. friends, feed posts) and choose to publish content. Those features are optional.

Retention & deletion

Data is kept while your account is active. You may delete individual items in the app. To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below.

Security

Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing. API access uses signed tokens. No system is perfectly secure; use a strong unique password.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes
